Who are you??

Welcome to my personal website! My name’s Alex Perry, pleasure to meet you. I’ve lived in a lot of places over the previous couple of years however I am currently settled in Staffordshire, a county in England.

I studied Law at University and graduated with a 2:1. Despite this, I’ve always had a huge passion for programming, web development and even a tiny bit of design! During my studies I soon realised that even though I loved the study of law, a career in web was where I saw myself in the future.

I’ve been freelancing since the age of around 16 (I’m 22 now), and spent a lot of time creating themes for the *Invision Power Board* software. I created and ran IPBSkinning.com which was highly successful during the boom of the forum system. One of my themes ‘Sylo’ is one of the most downloaded themes of all time for the platform.

I soon moved on from this and onto creating sites for the WordPress platform however it soon gets boring creating theme after theme on a daily basis – and that led me to where I am today….

Where do you work?

I currently work as a front-end web developer at Codeweavers in Stafford. Without sounding too much like an advertisement, we create awesome web applications for the motor industry. This includes clients such as Ford, Bmw, Suzuki, and brand car dealerships all over the UK.

Working at Codeweavers means I get to spend my days working with Html/Css/Javascript/PHP and other framework and technologies such as Angular.js. I have a huge thirst for knowledge and so I’m always eager to learn about the ‘next best thing’.
