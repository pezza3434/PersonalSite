Find out how old you really look.

HORU (how old are you) will answer the question you have asked yourself hundreds of times, how old do I look?

How does it work?

You upload a single, or number of, personal image/s of yourself and other people decide on how old they think you look; you in turn can vote on other peoples images. HORU then gives you a dashboard where you can see the averages of these votes. This results in the most reliable way of finding out how old other people think you look.

When can I use it?

HORU is still a work in progress. However, by entering your email on the right, we will keep you updated on the progress of the app and give you first access as soon as it is available.
